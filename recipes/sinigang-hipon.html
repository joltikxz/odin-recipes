Sinigang na Hipon

Description

Sinigang na Hipon sa Sampaloc or Filipino Shrimp Soup in tamarind broth is a quick and easy soup dish that you can prepare for lunch or dinner. This dish is quite flexible with the vegetable ingredients because you can mix and match your preferred sinigang veggies and you can also add some long green chili or finger pepper to make it bit spicy.

TThere are many ways to cook Sinigang na Hipon; this recipe is just one of them. For this recipe, common vegetables such as tomato and kangkong leaves are used. The good thing about this Sinigang na hipon version is its simplicity. There are minimal veggies involved, but still the outcome was good and I really find it to be comforting. I always feel some comfort after having warm sour soup even in warm weather..

Ingredients

  • 1/4 c. onion sliced
  • 1/4 c. tomatoes, chopped
  • 10 pcs. camias (lemon or calamansi or green "sampaloc" can be used to sour soup)
  • 6 c. rice water
  • 1/2 k. fresh shrimps, (med-sized)
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 2 c. kangkong leaves and tender stalks (swamp cabbage)

Steps

  1. Saute onion and tomatoes
  2. Add the camias and cook until tender and mushy
  3. Add the rice water
  4. Let boil
  5. When boiling, drop the shrimps
  6. Season with salt
  7. Add kangkong and cook 2 minutes
